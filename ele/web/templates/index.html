Features

What ele does before
you open Lightroom.

ele is a preprocessing engine, not an editor. It creates the latitude your editor needs to do its best work.

Highlight Recovery
Blown-out skies and windows hold detail instead of turning solid white — giving Lightroom’s Highlights slider something to actually work with.
Shadow Latitude
Deep shadows lift proportionally, not patchily — detail emerges cleanly from near-black without the muddy grey cast linear lifting creates.
ProPhoto RGB Color
A wider color space than sRGB or Adobe RGB. Rich colors that would clip on export are preserved and recoverable in Lightroom.
Color Headroom
Compressed, flat colors get a boost in the midtones while skin tones and bright areas are protected from looking artificial.
Print Upscaling
Neural super resolution scales to ×6 for large-format print. Print+ adds Replicate’s Clarity Upscaler for maximum edge sharpness and detail.
Lite Mode
For photographers who do their own heavy editing: export a flatter file with minimal preprocessing and interpret the tones yourself.
